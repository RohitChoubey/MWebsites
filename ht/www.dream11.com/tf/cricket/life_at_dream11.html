  • Team comes first. Always!

    We work and play like a world-class sports team, and believe that the team is always greater than the individual.

  • Be the best you can be

    We trust all our teammates to give their best. We encourage learning and empower our teammates to find their best way of working, learning and growing.

  • ‘A’ team - every time, everyday!

    We hire only A-players & their work speaks for itself. Our team includes former employees from Google, Netflix, Ola, InMobi, FedEx and many others.

  • Empowered to play any position

    At Dream11, we encourage people to learn and create their own niche. Experimentation is at the core of what we do & we empower unconventional thinkers to drive innovation.

  • 99% Data. 1% Gut.

    Data is the true democracy and we’re obsessed with it. All our team decisions are informed and backed by data. Everything else is secondary.

  • Play to win and lead

    We’ve had a lot of firsts here at Dream11:

    • India’s 1st Fantasy Sports platform
    • 1st to introduce Fantasy Kabaddi & NBA
    • 1st Fantasy Sports platform with 3 crore+ users
    • 1st Indian platform to offer over Rs.1 Cr per match

    We’re also the Official Fantasy Partner of Hero CPL T20 2017 & Hero ISL

  • A home turf like no other

    We have meticulously designed every aspect of our workplace. It’s not an office, it’s our home turf, where we play hard and work hard, striving to give our users an unparalleled playing experience.

  • Cutting-edge delivery with bleeding edge technology

    We use the latest technology such as Cassandra, Mongodb, AWS cloud components (Aurora, Redshift), Kafka, etc. to power Dream11. This is complemented by a PWA backed front end, and an Android & iOS app. And we’re only warming up!
