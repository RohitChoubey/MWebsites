How To Play Fantasy Football Games During WC Russia 2018

Football, footy, soccer - whatever you choose to call it, there’s no question that the beautiful game is the most passionately followed sport in the world. WC Football is hands down the most widely viewed and followed sporting event in the world. Since 1930, the tournament has seen footballers from different countries become all-time legends. This year, WC Football witnesses the participation of 32 national teams in the host country of Russia. Among the 32 teams, Iceland and Panama would be playing WC Football games for the first time. 64 matches, 12 venues, 11 cities - the WC Russia 2018 promises to be a thrilling tournament.

Creating your Dream11 Fantasy Football Team

While many fans are looking forward to watching the soccer games on TV, you can make your WC 2018 experience even more engaging with Fantasy Football games on Dream11. On Dream11, you can participate in the Fantasy Football version of the actual WC matches and compete against other soccer fans across the country. The process is incredibly simple. Here’s how you can experience Dream11 Fantasy Football for the Football WC 2018:

  • Create a Dream11 account using your email. You can also log in using your Facebook or Google profile.
  • Once logged in, you can explore upcoming cricket and football matches and join Fantasy Cricket and Football contests.
  • Head to the Fantasy Football section and select the WC football match you want to play.
  • It’s time to choose from both the playing teams in the following categories within the allocated 100 credits: GK - 1 Goalkeeper, DEF - 3-5 Defenders, MID - 3-5 Midfielders and ST - 1-3 Strikers. You can pick a maximum of seven (7) players from a single team
  • Select the Captain and Vice-captain of your Fantasy Football team. Your Captain will give you 2 times and your Vice-Captain will give you 1.5 times their original points respectively. So ensure that you pick players that you think will perform the best!
  • Want to experiment with different team combinations? You are free to create upto 6 teams and join up to 200 contests in every match on Dream11. If your selected team performs well, you stand to win BIG!
