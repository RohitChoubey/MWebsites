    Buy Kurtas and Kurtis Online

    Kurtas and kurtis are considered to be traditional or ethnic wear. What is the difference between kurtas and kurtis? They are essentially the same, except kurtis are shorter than kurtas. The primary criteria for selecting a fancy kurti is the need for one, i.e., is it for a formal occasion or a casual one. Next you can consider the fabric type, such as georgette, cotton, wool, etc. You can then choose the style of your kurta. There are different types of kurti designs & kinds of sleeves available for straight-cut kurtas, such as three-fourth sleeves, full sleeves, cap sleeves and even sleeveless. Let’s explore more to help you pick the right kurta!

    Kurtas for Different Occasions

    Latest Casual Kurtas and Kurtis Dresses
    For daily wear you’d probably want an outfit that is comfortable throughout the day. Who said can’t be fancy? From geometric patterns to asymmetric styles, you can easily shop for stylish kurtas online. Popular brands, such as  have an amazing collection of casual kurtas and kurtis. 
    Formal Kurtas
    What makes formal kurtas different from casual kurtas? These kurtas have more intricate designs and may feature patterns with sequins, beads and stones, and some embroidery. Also, these kurtas are more expensive than casual ones. Formal kurtas are ideal for parties, weddings, or interviews. Imara, Vishudh, Soch and Biba have beautiful formal kurtas.
    Slit Kurtas
    The name says it all, these kurtas have slits. These looks great when paired with ripped jeans, cigarette pants and skirts. Front-slit and side-slit kurtas are the most popular Some brands that have a great collection of slit kurtas are Libas, Biba and Global Desi. 

    Kurta Material
    Kurtas and kurtis come in different fabrics, such as chiffon, cotton and georgette, wool. Let’s see what these materials are:

    Cotton: Cotton fabric is the most preferred and popular fabric. This fabric allows your skin to breathe and is particularly useful during summers. Itcan also be blended with other fabrics.

    Silk-blend: Silk can be blended with other fabrics, such as cotton, satin and rayon. These are ideal for grand occasions, such as engagements, weddings or formal parties.

    Georgette is a lightweight crepe fabric. Kurtas made of georgette fabric are flowy and flatter a woman’s body.

    Chiffon is a plain-woven, lightweight fabric that is made from cotton or silk.

    Wool: Wool is a cloth fiber obtained from the thick coats of either sheep or goats. Don’t let the cold weather dampen your sense of style as  keep you warm and also look great.
