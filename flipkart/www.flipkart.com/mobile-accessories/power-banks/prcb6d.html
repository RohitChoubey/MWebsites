    Power Bank - FAQs


    1. How do I know which power bank to buy?

    You must always buy a power bank that has a higher capacity compared to what your mobile phone battery has. For example, if your smartphone has a battery capacity of 3000 mAh, a power bank with 2200 mAh will not be able to fully charge your phone. Whereas, if you are using a power bank with a higher capacity, for example, a Sony battery bank with 3000 mAh capacity will easily full charge your phone.


    2. How many times can a battery bank charge my phone?

    The number of times a power bank can charge your phone depends on its capacity, remaining power in the power bank and the power supply.


    3. What is the lifespan of a power bank?

    A well-maintained power bank can retain up to 80-90% of its original capacity after 400-500 cycles of charge and discharge.


    4. Which is the best brand of battery bank for smartphones?

    You must always look at a power bank that is fast, compact, plug-and-go has enough capacity for several charges and is super-tough. Another important factor to consider is the battery capacity of your phone. Always buy a power bank that has higher capacity than your mobile phone.


    5. Which is the best power bank for iPhones?

    While buying a power/ battery bank for your iPhone you must consider a few factors including capacity and portability. There are chargers from good brands like the Kodak 15000 mAh power bank, Wayona 7000 mAh power bank and the like that can be used to easily charge iPhone 6. These models are slim and compact and easily fit into your jeans pocket. iPhone 7 can be charged using a UIMI U8 Ultra high capacity with 2A 15600 mAh power bank, Reliable RBL 005 with 2USB ports and torch 13000 mAh power bank and more.
