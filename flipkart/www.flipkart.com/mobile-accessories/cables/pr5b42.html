    Buy Mobile Cables Online at Flipkart.com


    Cables are an inevitable part of every gadget. Whether it is something as big as television, or as small as a mobile phone, you need them to get the basic work done quickly and efficiently. Carrying one with yourself wherever you go, or keeping one at home or work desk is always a good idea, as it comes handy in a variety of ways.


    There are different types of cables, which perform different functions. They differ in terms of function, feature and compatibility. Below are some of the popular types explained in simple words, so you can choose one that will serve your purpose the best.


    USB cable


    This is the most popular one out of the lot. The main function of these cables is the fact that they can help transfer files at a high speed, while also charging your phone. Make sure the size of the port supports your mobile phone’s port, and fits properly.


    Power sharing cable


    The cable used to connect a smartphone or a tablet to another device, which has a micro USB for charging, is called a power sharing cable. This just means that both the devices share the power.


    OTG cable


    OTG cables are used to connect the mobile devices to peripheral accessories like keyboards, mice and USB drives directly. For instance, if you want to play a game on your tablet, you can attach a keyboard to it with the help of an OTG cable, and you can have a seamless gaming experience.


    AUX cable


    Listening to music will be a piece of cake with an AUX cable at your disposal. All you have to do is connect it to a stereo’s input, and enjoy listening to music or watching a movie.


    Lightning cable


    Created by Apple Inc., this cable is used to link Apple devices like iPhones, Pods and iPads to desktops, cameras and other peripheral devices.


    HDMI cable


    Now transmitting audio and video files to digital display devices will be a piece of cake with an HDMI cable coming to your rescue.


    Headphone splitter


    Many a time you like to watch a movie or listen to songs with your loved one while travelling. In such cases, a headphone splitter allows you to connect it to the laptop, and watch the movie with comfort without having to share the same headphone.


    Network cable


    This cable is used to link two networking devices to share files and perform other functions. For instance, you can connect computers to share the printer and scanner access with the help of a network cable.


    Buying cables online


    Now you can buy cables online after reading the reviews, and finding out the right prices in India. Just choose one which will be compatible with your device, and which will fulfill your purpose of buy, and add it to your home and office.
