Our Services

Hair

From cool haircuts to classy, contemporary hairstyles to unique styles for curly, wavy, or straight hair, our hair artists offer some of the trendiest and most stylish hairstyle that compliments your personality and aspirations. Feel free to get in touch for advice on the best haircut for you.

Beauty

We offer a range of treatment to enhance your holistic beauty - be it for the face, body or even your mind.  Our experts just want you to enjoy your beauty therapies.  So come, relax and turn into a better looking person.

Bridal

We have a dedicated team of passionate professionals who are ready to groom you up for your wedding day.  Gallant is the only-of-its-kind salon in the region having two separate bridal rooms with attached powder room and waiting lounge. Services will be delivered as per your choice in salon or off site at your home or wedding venue.

Pedicure

Experience your feet at their finest. We have trained beauty therapist who will let you discover the best quality treatments for your feet.

Nail

We offer a full array of nail services from a simple manicure to custom crafted nail enhancements and specialized 3d adornments.

Make Up

Our expertise in makeup services is unparalleled. We are known for rendering world-class make up services complimented by latest techniques with premium products.

Our skills
